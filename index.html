How scoring works

Each colleague is assigned one country at random. You stay in the game as long as your team is still in the tournament — the last team standing (whoever's country goes furthest) wins the prize. While the group stage is on, the live leaderboard ranks everyone by their team's points and goal difference so there's always a pecking order. Scores update automatically from the live FIFA World Cup feed.
